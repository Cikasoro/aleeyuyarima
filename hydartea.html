Hydar Tea
brand strategy

Keywords

Natural, Refreshing, Healthy, Premium, Flavorful

The Client

Hydar Tea is a premium tea brand dedicated to providing health-conscious consumers with natural, flavorful beverages. It was founded on the belief that tea can be both nourishing and delicious.

Logo Design

The logo design for Hydar Tea combines elements of nature and elegant geometry to reflect the brand's commitment to quality and health. The use of a tea leaf icon emphasizes the natural ingredients, while the serif typography conveys a sense of trust.

Color Palette

The color palette for Hydar Tea was carefully selected to evoke feelings of freshness, health, and tranquility. The primary colors include a soothing green, a warm beige, and a rich brown, which together create a harmonious and inviting aesthetic.
